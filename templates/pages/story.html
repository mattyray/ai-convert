My Journey

Six years ago, I dove into shallow water and broke my neck. I was paralyzed from the chest down. Before that day, I was a commercial fisherman—working hard, living wild, and braving storms at sea. That day changed everything. What followed was pain, isolation, and a complete loss of control over my body and life.

But that wasn't the end of my story. I taught myself how to use a computer with a stylus and voice commands. I learned to code, to build applications, to write, to create. I rebuilt my life from scratch. What began as survival became purpose: helping others through web development, writing, and art.

Today, I develop professional Django applications, create digital art, and share my journey of resilience. My story is proof that even in the darkest places, light can emerge — if we choose to create it.
