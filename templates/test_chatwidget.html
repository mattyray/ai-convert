🧪 Chatbot Test & Training Guide

This is a test of a custom Django-based AI chat widget created by Matt Raynor.

📁 How does it work?

The assistant is powered by OpenAI, but you control what it knows by placing .json files in:

django-chatwidget/chatwidget/data/

Each file should contain one or more entries like:

[
  {"title": "About Matt", "content": "Matt is a quadriplegic developer and creator."},
  {"title": "Hiring", "content": "You can hire Matt for freelance web development."}
]

💡 What kind of content can you add?

  • FAQs about your site
  • Info about your services or story
  • Troubleshooting steps
  • Anything you want the chatbot to say

🤖 Ask it things like:

  • "How do I train this chatbot?"
  • "Who is Matt Raynor?"
  • "How do I add a knowledge file?"
  • "Can I hire Matt?"
